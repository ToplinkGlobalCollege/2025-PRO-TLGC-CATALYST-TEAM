# Medium Motor
![medium](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5ef2ff19-d5e1-4ada-b066-65c2ceaa297c)
The EV3 Medium Motor is a smaller and faster servo motor used in LEGO MINDSTORMS. It is designed for lighter tasks that require quicker, more precise movements. It has a built-in rotation sensor but produces less torque, making it ideal for mechanisms like arms or tools.
# Large Motor
![large motor](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5340a849-69f2-48c2-a070-a0ca8ac7edd2)
The EV3 Large Motor is a powerful servo motor used in LEGO MINDSTORMS. It provides precise control of rotation, with built-in rotation sensors for accurate feedback, and is designed for driving wheels, gears, and other mechanical parts in robotics projects.

# Pixy
![pixy](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2024ef7c-7071-4e03-9acd-6753681afbc9)
The Pixy Camera, often called Pixy or Pixy2, is a smart vision sensor used in robotics. It can detect and track colored objects, lines, barcodes, and simple shapes in real time. Unlike standard cameras, Pixy processes images onboard and sends object data (like position, size, and color) directly to a robot’s controller, such as the EV3 brick, using SPI, I2C, UART, or analog/digital signals.
# Ev3 Intelligent Brick
![lego-45500-ev3-intelligent-brick](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d4b93bbb-b4cc-4008-a0e1-320f307a0089)
The EV3 Intelligent Brick is the programmable computer (or “brain”) of the LEGO MINDSTORMS EV3 system. It controls motors and sensors, runs programs, connects via USB, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi, and features a display screen, buttons, speaker, and ports for inputs and outputs.
